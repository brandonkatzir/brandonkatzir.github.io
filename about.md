I’m the Systems and Discovery Librarian at Smith College in Northampton, Massachusetts. 

Before working in academic libraries, I was an Associate Professor of English at Oklahoma City University. I received my Ph.D from Louisiana State University in 2017. My research focuses on the history of modern English, the dictionaries and style guides of the nineteenth century, and Jews in nineteenth and twentieth century Anglophone culture. I’ve been published in journals like _Literature Compass_, _Rhetorica_, _Philosophy & Rhetoric_, and _Jewish History_.

## digital projects

Co-creator, [Victorian Jewish Writers Project (VJWP)](https://victorianjewishwritersproject.org). The Victorian Jewish Writers Project is a digital collection focusing on Anglo-Jewish writing during the long nineteenth century. VJWP also has a [development blog](https://blog.victorianjewishwritersproject.org) for catalog and technical updates.
